Welcome to DrChrono Kiosk

DrChrono Kiosk is a self-check-in kiosk with integration to the DrChrono EHR. DrChrono Kiosk allows patients to self check-in and indicates to healthcare providers how long patients have been waiting.

Sign in with DrChrono

Improved Patient Experience

Kiosk supports patient self check-in and self verification of patient details, easing the check-in process and improving patients' experiences.

Doctor Dashboard

Kiosk offers an easy to use dashboard which displays all of today's appointments and allows physicians to quickly ascertain how many patients have been waiting and for how long. Appointments can be easily started and completed from the dashboard.

Office Metrics

Kiosk Metrics offer an overview on various key metrics on office/physician efficiency and patient quality, including average patient wait time, average patient tardiness, appointment efficiency, and schedule pace integrity.
